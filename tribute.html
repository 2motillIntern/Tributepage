10 Things to Know About Bronzeville

  • Nicknamed fondly Chicago's Black Metropolis
  • Bronzeville sits in a premiere location adjacent and bordering neighborhoods such as, Hyde Park and Kenwood.
  • Located on Chicago's Southside and just 4 miles from the South Loo
  • Bronzeville was the economic and cultural hub for Chicago's Black community.
  • 35th street became the central loction for commerce in Bronzeville, due to segregation.
  • Bronzeville is home to a few legends such as: Nat King Cole, Quincy Jones, and Gwendolyn Brooks
